What's New at NMC?

On Oct. 18, 2018, one of the strongest storms in history destroyed nearly all of Northern Marianas College and the Northern Mariana Islands. Seven years later, the College cuts the ribbon on the first of five new buildings.

This mini documentary is dedicated to each and every individual, company, and agency whose contribution and support have made the ribbon cutting possible and who continue to fuel NMC's recovery efforts.
